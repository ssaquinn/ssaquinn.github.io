###Emotional intelligence### >Emotional intelligence is the ability to understand, process and express emotions in a appropriate and constructive way. It also compasses the ability to recognise and have empathy towards others emotions and situations. ###Emotional Intelligence vs Intellect### >Intellect (not IQ -as these have been shown to be an inappropriate and biased test) can be seen as the brains ability to process information. Similar to emotional intelligence, however it deals with more physical/practical forms of data. Where as emotional intelligence is focused on the processing of emotional data. ###The Importance of Emotional Intelligence### >Emotional intelligence is important in regulating *Self Management *Self Awareness *Social Awareness *Relationship Management. > >Through these attributes, you are able to take care of your own wellbeing and communicate issues effectivley. This allows you to be a reliable member of a team. You are also able to empathize and communicate clearly with others allowing for positive interactions, problem solving and conflict resolution.
